This is a voluntary and volatile final step to the exercise! To prevent the player from becoming too powerful, we shall now limit the total sum of all three characteristics to 10. This can be achieved in many different ways, but will include:

  • Making the case in the reducer first set the intended characteristic, and then potentially lower the others
  • Making the UI updater not only set the spans, but also the sliders themselves
  • Make sure, when you lower another characteristic to compensate, that you don't go below 1!

Additonally we should make the UI show how many points remain. Note that this should not be part of the state, but merely calculated in the UI updater.
